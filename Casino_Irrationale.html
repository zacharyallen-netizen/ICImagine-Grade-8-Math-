Mission Briefing — know the tells

Rational

Integers, fractions, terminating or repeating decimals, and roots of perfect squares & cubes (e.g., √16 = 4).

Irrational

Decimals that never repeat and never end — √ of non-perfect squares, ∛ of non-perfect cubes, and π.

Operations

Rational ± irrational is always irrational; a non-zero rational × irrational is irrational. But irrational × irrational (or ±) can land either way — do the math first.
