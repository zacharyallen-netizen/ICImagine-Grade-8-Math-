2.2 Scientific Notation

🔑 FORMAT: a × 10n where 1 ≤ a < 10 (only ONE digit before decimal!)
  • Large numbers → Positive exponent (move decimal LEFT): 45,000 = 4.5 × 10⁴
  • Small numbers → Negative exponent (move decimal RIGHT): 0.0067 = 6.7 × 10⁻³

⭐ MULTIPLYING Scientific Notation

🔑 MULTIPLY RULE:
1. MULTIPLY the coefficients (the a-values)
2. ADD the exponents
3. Adjust if coefficient is not between 1 and 10

Example: (3 × 10⁴) × (2 × 10⁵)

Multiply coefficients: 3 × 2 = 6
ADD exponents: 4 + 5 = 9
Answer: 6 × 10⁹ ✓ (no adjustment needed)

⭐ DIVIDING Scientific Notation

🔑 DIVIDE RULE:
1. DIVIDE the coefficients (the a-values)
2. SUBTRACT the exponents
3. Adjust if coefficient is not between 1 and 10

Example: (8 × 10⁶) ÷ (4 × 10²)

Divide coefficients: 8 ÷ 4 = 2
SUBTRACT exponents: 6 - 2 = 4
Answer: 2 × 10⁴ ✓

⚠️ Adjustment Rules (Know BEFORE doing problems!)

⚠️ ADJUSTMENT RULES:
• If coefficient ≥ 10: Move decimal LEFT, ADD 1 to exponent
• If coefficient < 1: Move decimal RIGHT, SUBTRACT 1 from exponent

Example needing adjustment: (5 × 10³) × (4 × 10²)

Multiply coefficients: 5 × 4 = 20
ADD exponents: 3 + 2 = 5
Result: 20 × 10⁵
⚠️ 20 is NOT between 1 and 10!
Adjust: 20 = 2.0 × 10¹, so add 1 to exponent
Final: 2.0 × 10⁶ ✓

Example needing adjustment: (3.6 × 10⁸) ÷ (9 × 10²)

Divide coefficients: 3.6 ÷ 9 = 0.4
SUBTRACT exponents: 8 - 2 = 6
Result: 0.4 × 10⁶
⚠️ 0.4 is NOT between 1 and 10!
Adjust: 0.4 = 4 × 10⁻¹, so subtract 1 from exponent
Final: 4 × 10⁵ ✓

Word Problems with Scientific Notation

🚀 Problem: Space Travel

Distance to Mars: 2.25 × 10⁸ km. Spacecraft speed: 5 × 10⁴ km/hr. How long to reach Mars?
Time = Distance ÷ Speed
Time = (2.25 × 10⁸) ÷ (5 × 10⁴)
Divide: 2.25 ÷ 5 = 0.45
Subtract exponents: 8 - 4 = 4
Result: 0.45 × 10⁴ → Adjust to 4.5 × 10³
Answer: 4.5 × 10³ hours (4,500 hours) ✓

🦠 Problem: Bacteria Growth

A petri dish has 3 × 10⁴ bacteria. Each bacterium splits into 2, and this happens 4 times. How many bacteria total?
Multiply by 2 four times = 2⁴ = 16
(3 × 10⁴) × 16 = (3 × 10⁴) × (1.6 × 10¹)
Multiply: 3 × 1.6 = 4.8
Add exponents: 4 + 1 = 5
Answer: 4.8 × 10⁵ bacteria ✓

💻 Problem: Computer Storage

A hard drive has 2 × 10¹² bytes. A photo is 5 × 10⁶ bytes. How many photos can it store?
Photos = Total ÷ Photo size
Photos = (2 × 10¹²) ÷ (5 × 10⁶)
Divide: 2 ÷ 5 = 0.4
Subtract exponents: 12 - 6 = 6
Result: 0.4 × 10⁶ → Adjust to 4 × 10⁵
Answer: 4 × 10⁵ photos (400,000 photos) ✓

📝 Review: Worked Examples & Error Analysis

📝 Simplify: (4x³y²)(3x⁵y)

Step 1: Multiply coefficients: 4 × 3 = 12
Step 2: Add x exponents: x³ · x⁵ = x⁸
Step 3: Add y exponents: y² · y¹ = y³
Step 4: 12x⁸y³ ✓

📝 Multiply: (3.2 × 10⁵)(4 × 10³)

Step 1: Multiply coefficients: 3.2 × 4 = 12.8
Step 2: Add exponents: 10⁵ × 10³ = 10⁸
Step 3: Combine: 12.8 × 10⁸
Step 4: Adjust (12.8 not between 1-10): 1.28 × 10⁹ ✓

🔍 Spot the Error: Simplify (3x²)³

✗ Student work: (3x²)³ = 3x⁶
✓ Fix: The coefficient 3 must also be cubed: 3³ = 27. Correct: 27x⁶
💡 When raising a power to a power, the coefficient gets the exponent too: (3x²)³ = 3³ · x²·³ = 27x⁶

🔍 Spot the Error: Convert 0.0045 to scientific notation

✗ Student work: 0.0045 → move decimal 3 right → 4.5 × 10³
✓ Fix: Small number → NEGATIVE exponent. Correct: 4.5 × 10⁻³
💡 Numbers less than 1 always have negative exponents in scientific notation.
