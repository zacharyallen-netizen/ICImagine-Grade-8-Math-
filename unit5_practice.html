🔍 Common Mistakes

Can you spot what went wrong?

Find c: legs 6 and 8
✗ Student: Student writes: c² = 100, so c = 50
✓ Fix: WRONG! Forgot to take the square root. c = √100 = 10
💡 Last step is ALWAYS the square root.
Find leg: c=13, leg=5
✗ Student: Student writes: b² = 169+25 = 194
✓ Fix: WRONG! Finding a LEG means SUBTRACT: b² = 169−25 = 144, b = 12
💡 Hypotenuse → ADD squares. Leg → SUBTRACT squares.

⚡ Key Tips for This Unit

✅ Hypotenuse = longest side, opposite the right angle.
✅ Finding c (hyp): ADD, then square root. Finding a leg: SUBTRACT, then square root.
✅ Know your triples: 3-4-5, 5-12-13, 8-15-17, 7-24-25.
✅ Distance formula IS the Pythagorean theorem on a grid.
