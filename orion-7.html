How to Play

You're an astronaut aboard the Orion-7 research station. What starts as a routine shift turns into something you didn't sign up for. Every situation requires a scientific notation calculation to proceed.

Answering Problems

  • Scientific notation: Two boxes — coefficient (like 3.2) and exponent (like -6).
  • Standard form: One box — type the number with or without commas (450,000 or 450000).
  • Comparison: Click the option you think is correct.
  • Press Enter to submit.

Oxygen Meter

  • You start at 100%.
  • Wrong answers cost 8%.
  • Hints cost 3%.
  • Keep more than 40% at the end for the clean ending.

Stuck? Click Show Answer after a wrong attempt — it's free.
