How to Play

Move your shape (teal) onto the target outline (gold dashed) using the four transformation tools.

The Tools

  • Translate → Drag your shape to a new spot. It snaps to whole-number coordinates when you let go.
  • Reflect ⇋ Pick which line to reflect over: the x-axis, the y-axis, y = x, or y = −x.
  • Rotate ↻ Rotate around the origin by 90° counterclockwise, 90° clockwise, or 180°.
  • Dilate ⤢ Scale from the origin by ½, ⅓, 2, or 3.

Winning a Puzzle

As soon as your shape's vertices match the target's, the shape turns green and you advance to the next puzzle.

Stuck?

Click Hint to reveal the next step in one valid solution. You can also click Reset to return to the starting position. Hints and resets don't hurt you — they're for learning.

Difficulty Levels

  • Level 1: One transformation per puzzle.
  • Level 2: Two transformations in sequence.
  • Level 3: Three transformations — composition practice.
