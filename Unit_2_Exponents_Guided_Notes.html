Modeling the Rules — See WHY

Each rule below comes from just writing out the repeated multiplication. Click a card to model it with factors — first base, second base, result.

Product Rule · x³ × x² = x⁵

See why → click to model the rule
xxx × xx  =  xxxxx  =  x⁵
Count the factors: 3 + 2 = 5. Same base → ADD the exponents.

Quotient Rule · x⁵ ÷ x² = x³

See why → click to model the rule
xxxxx ÷ xx  → cancel 2 pairs →  xxx = x³
Cancel matching factors: 5 − 2 = 3. Same base → SUBTRACT the exponents.

Power Rule · (x²)³ = x⁶

See why → click to model the rule
x²x²x² = xx·xx·xx = x⁶
Three copies of x²: 2 × 3 = 6. Power to a power → MULTIPLY.

Zero Exponent · x⁰ = 1

See why → click to model the rule
xxx ÷ xxx = 1,  but by the quotient rule = x3−3 = x⁰. So x⁰ = 1
Anything (except 0) divided by itself is 1 → x⁰ = 1.

Negative Exponent · x⁻³ = 1/x³

See why → click to model the rule
xx ÷ xxxxx = x2−5 = x⁻³;  cancelling leaves 1 / x³
A negative exponent means ‘flip it’ → x⁻ⁿ = 1/xⁿ.

• If coefficient < 1: Move decimal RIGHT, SUBTRACT 1 from exponent

D Word Problems

Strategy: Identify what operation to use. Write your sci notation setup. Adjust if needed.
1. The Sun is about 9.3 × 10⁷ miles from Earth. Light travels 1.86 × 10⁵ miles per second. How many seconds does it take light to reach Earth?
(9.3 × 10⁷) ÷ (1.86 × 10⁵) = 5 × 10² seconds (500 seconds)
2. A bacteria cell is about 2 × 10⁻⁶ m long. A virus is about 5 × 10⁻⁸ m long. How many times longer is the bacteria?
(2 × 10⁻⁶) ÷ (5 × 10⁻⁸) = 0.4 × 10² = 40 times longer
3. The US national debt is about 3.4 × 10¹³ dollars. The US population is about 3.4 × 10⁸ people. What is the debt per person?
(3.4 × 10¹³) ÷ (3.4 × 10⁸) = 1 × 10⁵ = $100,000 per person
4. A grain of sand has mass 6.5 × 10⁻⁴ grams. A beach has about 8 × 10¹⁸ grains of sand. What is the total mass in grams?
(6.5 × 10⁻⁴)(8 × 10¹⁸) = 52 × 10¹⁴ = 5.2 × 10¹⁵ grams
5. There are about 3.7 × 10¹³ cells in a human body. Each cell is about 1 × 10⁻⁵ m wide. If all cells were lined up, how long in meters?
(3.7 × 10¹³)(1 × 10⁻⁵) = 3.7 × 10⁸ meters
6. A computer does 4.8 × 10⁹ calculations per second. A task needs 1.44 × 10¹² calculations. How many seconds to finish?
(1.44 × 10¹²) ÷ (4.8 × 10⁹) = 0.3 × 10³ = 3 × 10² seconds (300 seconds)

★ Answers

2.1 Exponent Rules

Product: x³×x⁴=x⁷; (2x³)(3x²)=6x⁵; "Same base? ADD." Quotient: x⁶÷x²=x⁴; (12x⁷)÷(3x²)=4x⁵; "Same base? SUBTRACT." Power: (x²)³=x⁶; (2x³)²=4x⁶; "Power to power? MULTIPLY." Zero: 5⁰=1; (3x⁵)⁰=1; "ANY base⁰=1." Negative: 2⁻³=1/8; 4x⁻³=4/x³; "Flip to fraction." −3²=−9 vs (−3)²=9. Worked: (4x³y²)(3x⁵y)=12x⁸y³. Spot the error: (3x²)³=27x⁶.

2.2 Scientific Notation

Large → positive exp; small → negative exp. Multiply: MULTIPLY coefficients, ADD exponents, adjust to 1–10. Divide: DIVIDE coefficients, SUBTRACT exponents. (3×10⁴)(2×10⁵)=6×10⁹. (8×10⁶)÷(4×10²)=2×10⁴. (5×10³)(4×10²)=20×10⁵ → 2.0×10⁶. Spot the error: 0.0045 = 4.5×10⁻³.
